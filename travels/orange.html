Orange Travels is a popular bus service provider known for its reliable and comfortable travel options across various cities in India. They offer a wide range of buses, including luxurious AC sleeper coaches, semi-sleeper, and non-AC buses to meet different traveler needs. Each bus is designed for comfort with spacious seats, ample legroom, and cleanliness standards, providing a relaxing journey. Orange Travels also prioritizes safety with experienced drivers and well-maintained buses. Additionally, they provide convenient online booking, timely schedules, and onboard amenities like blankets, charging points, and sometimes WiFi, making it a preferred choice for long-distance travel.
